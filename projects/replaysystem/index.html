Replay-System in Unity

During my master thesis I developed a replay system specialized in a Multi-User VR-Environment. I used an already existing 3D-scenario from my work as a research assistent. The System is non-deterministic, which records the game states in a fixed interval and interpolates between them, other than just recording the inputs and simulating the game. The System also supports two optimization algorithms known from Quake 3 and Braid. You can see full details of my integration in my blog post. The Replay-System features also multiple timelines, so you can record several alternativ scenarios in one file.
