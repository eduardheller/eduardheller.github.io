Battle of the Throne

Battle of the Thrones is a 3D towerdefense game made in C# and OpenGL. I wrote my own engine and shaders to acomplish this. It supports:

  • shadow mapping/cascade shadow mapping
  • view frustum culling
  • water refraction/reflection
  • stack based GUI
  • instance based particles
  • distance field text rendering
  • …


Source code: https://github.com/eduardheller/towerdefense/tree/master/TowerDefense
